From Georgetown

  • Take I-35 south and take the Exit for Westinghouse Road.
  • On the access road you will come to a stop sign and continue south on the access road.
  • You will see a large brown facility on the right called "Teco-Westinghouse".
  • You will turn right into the Teco Westinghouse complex using the south entrance to the parking lot.
  • Park in the south lot only which is the first parking lot on your left.
  • Follow the sidewalk across the footbridge into the building.
  • Protect America is the first door on the right, Suite C.
  • Call 512-218-8833, ext 8245 if you have questions.
  • Protect America, Inc. 5100 N. IH-35, Suite B, Round Rock, TX 78681
