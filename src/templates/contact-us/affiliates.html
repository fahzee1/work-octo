The following rules of engagement are to be followed:

  1. Any affiliate who violates this policy will be terminated immediately without warning. Affiliates who want to bid on search engines for the purpose of this affiliate program must own and operate their own URL and drive the traffic to their own URL. The URL that affiliates own and operate must contain appropriate content and cannot be a redirect URL.
  2. Negative broad matches must be applied to any PPC activities you are conducting on our behalf on the word "Protect America".
  3. No "Exact" or "Broad" match bidding on the trademark term "Protect America" and derivatives of its spelling.
  4. No "Term Misspelling" bidding on the trademark term "Protect America" and derivatives of its spelling.
  5. No "Broad" match bidding on any combinations of that include the following keyword terms: Protect, America.
  6. No "Phrase" match bidding on the following keyword term grouping: Protect America.
  7. No bidding upon or targeting of the keyword "Protect America" and derivatives of its spelling for other competitive offerings, such as those offered by ADT, Honeywell, and others.
  8. No contextual marketing efforts of any kind may be focused or targeted through a keyword match on the keyword "Protect America" and derivatives of its spelling.
  9. No attempts at cyber squatting through the misspelling or use of Protect America trademarks and their derivatives within URL strings and domain names.
