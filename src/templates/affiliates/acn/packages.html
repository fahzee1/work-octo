Monitoring Options

Landline

The standard connection for most traditional security systems. Landline monitoring easily connects right into your current phone line. This is the perfect solution for homes and businesses that are in cell dead-zones or lack high speed internet.

Included with Standard Packages

Broadband

No phone? No problem. Use your modem or router to connect to the monitoring facility instead. This high-speed option is remarkably fast! The signal from your home or business to our monitoring facility is received within 2 seconds. This option is perfect for Digital Phone customers.

Cellular

This solution is completely wireless. Simply plug the command station into a power outlet and every other piece of equipment communicates wirelessly. No landline phone or internet is required since all communication is via cellular towers.
