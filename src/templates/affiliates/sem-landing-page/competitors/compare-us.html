Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why Protect America?

    Security is extremely important, but it shouldn’t be expensive. Protect America has secured over half a million homes in all 50 states because our wireless technology is affordable, simple, interactive and user-friendly. We’ve perfected security and automation and have made it accessible for anyone seeking to protect their home and family without breaking the bank. We’re happy to help explore your best options and will match any competitor price with our Lowest Price Guarantee!

  • What if there’s a problem with my equipment?

    While other companies might charge hundreds of dollars to replace equipment, Protect America offers a Lifetime Replacement Plan that covers all equipment. If equipment breaks or malfunctions, we replace it. Period.

  • What is the biggest difference between Protect America and other companies?

    Price, flexibility and convenience. Security should be available for everyone, so we make it attainable within any budget. Not only does every package come with free equipment, monthly monitoring costs begin at only $19.99. Find a better price? We’ll match it with our lowest price guarantee. We also make customizing security a breeze. Swap equipment as you like, add fire protection and home automation – whatever makes sense for your home. Our DIY security packages save you the hassle of paying expensive installation fees with other companies. Simply open your package, call our designated security experts, and we’ll walk you through the installation process which usually takes less than an hour!

  • Do you offer home automation?

    Yes! Home automation features are easily added to your security package. They make a safe home even safer, and much more convenient. Home automation simply means making more parts of your house digital and under your command from a mobile device. Door locks and even lights are now controllable from anywhere – making life easier but also more secure.

  • What if I secure my home and then have to move?

    No problem. Protect America’s wireless technology and easy move policy make moving a breeze. We’ll even send you a kit to help re-install your system. All sensors and devices are wireless (not hardwired) and don’t require special equipment or a professional to remove and place in another home. Need help? Give us a call. We will walk you through it, step by step. Other companies charge fees to move your system to a new residence. Not so with Protect America. Call us and we’ll make sure your new address is monitored.

  • Is service available everywhere?

    With Protect America it is. We’re a national home security company serving all 50 states and Canada since 1992. Many security companies aren’t available everywhere and won’t serve some areas.
