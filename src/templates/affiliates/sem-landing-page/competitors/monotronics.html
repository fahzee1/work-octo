    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What makes Protect America different?

      Price and convenience. We believe everyone should be able to protect themselves without enormous payments and unnecessary fees. It’s how we’ve secured over half a million homes in the U.S. and Canada. Convenience and flexibility means being able to select what you want and leave out what you don’t need: instead of being confined to mandatory packages. We’ll help you create a plan that fits your home and lifestyle, because that’s the way it should be.

    • What if I move?

      No problem. Just give us a call and we’ll update your information, plus take you through the steps to move your equipment. We’ll even send you a moving kit! Protect America never charges its customers to move service to a new home or requires a new contract to be signed. Some other companies require you to sign a new contract of at least 36 months if you move to a new residence.

    • What if there’s a problem with my equipment?

      If equipment breaks or malfunctions, we replace it. Period. While other providers might charge hundreds of dollars to replace equipment, Protect America offers a Lifetime Replacement Plan that covers all equipment.

    • What is the installation process like?

      We’ve developed a unique process that avoids needless installation fees and having strange technicians walking through your home. The beauty of Protect America technology is that it’s wireless and pre-programmed by our specialists, so forget running wires or complicated tasks. Once a security expert has worked with you to create a plan for your house, we ship a custom security system right to your door. Then, when you have some time, call us and one of our installation professionals will take you through the process step-by-step. On average, it takes less than an hour. We’ll help you place your system, and then test to make sure everything is working correctly. Wireless technology is extremely user-friendly, and doesn’t need a professional to install.

    • What is home automation?

      Home automation is the future of home life. It’s safety and convenience wrapped into one. This technology allows you to control door locks, your security system, lights, appliances and more – all from a smartphone, tablet or computer. Not only is it convenient, it’s a way to stay more connected to your home. Receive text messages in the event of an alarm, view live footage on your phone with wireless cameras and always be in the driver’s seat.

    • What happens when my alarm system is triggered? How will the monitoring station respond?

      Our monitoring station will attempt to reach you. If you do not answer, we will dispatch the local Police to the monitoring address and will attempt to reach your emergency contacts.

    • How do I Arm and Disarm my new alarm system?

      Simply press one button to arm your system, and enter your master code to disarm it. Protect America alarm systems are also interactive, so you can arm and disarm from a smartphone with our free "SMART Connect" app!
