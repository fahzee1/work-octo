Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why home security and a medical panic?

    We understand the importance of safety for you and your loved ones. That's why Protect America helps with accidents and burglaries alike. Our technology is designed to improve the quality of home and life. The Protect America security network goes beyond home alarms - it helps safeguard loved ones too!

  • What happens when there is an emergency and I press the button?

    There are two things that happen:

    A. Your alarm will alert the local authorities immediately for dispatch.
    B. After dispatch to the property, our monitoring station will attempt to reach your listed family / emergency contacts.

  • What if I accidentally press the medical panic button?

    We take every emergency seriously. When the button is pressed your alarm will begin to ring and the monitoring station will dispatch the authorities. We then attempt to reach you and your listed emergency contacts. If a false alarm is established, the authorities will be notified and canceled.

  • How do I send a panic signal?

    Besides the medical panic pendant, every security system comes with one button emergency buttons. To send an Emergency, Fire or Police panic signal simply press and hold the appropriate button for 2 seconds, (Cross = Emergency, Fire = Fire, Police badge= Police).

  • Can I get the medical panic wet?

    Yes, the medical panic pendant and watch are waterproof! This feature is important since your loved one should keep this device close at all times.

  • What happens when my alarm system is triggered? How will the monitoring station respond?

    If your system is not disarmed after one minute, our monitoring station will attempt to reach you. If you do not answer, we will dispatch the local Police to the monitoring address and will attempt to reach your emergency contacts.

  • How do I Arm and Disarm my new alarm system?

    Simply press one button to arm your system, and enter your master code to disarm it. Protect America alarm systems are also interactive, so you can arm and disarm from a smartphone with our free "SMART Connect" app!
