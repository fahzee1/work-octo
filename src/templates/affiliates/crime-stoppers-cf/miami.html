About Crime Stoppers and Crime Stoppers Case Files

Founded by a police detective in Albuquerque in 1975, Crime Stoppers has grown into one of the most successful crime prevention initiatives in history. Today, nearly every major city in the world has a Crime Stoppers phone line to collect anonymous tips. Over the past 35 years, Crime Stoppers tip lines have helped law enforcement solve over one million cases and lead to the recovery of more than $5 billion in stolen property.

In many cities, Crime Stoppers tips that lead to an arrest boast a 95 percent conviction rate. Getting local communities to engage Crime Stoppers tip lines is an important effort that could have a dramatic impact on crime. Protect America is proud to sponsor Crime Stoppers Case Files, a syndicated TV show highlighting crimes that are important to specific local communities. Many major cities, like Miami and Los Angeles, have their own Casefiles program that gives prominent local crimes a higher profile and helps improve clearance rates.
