FAQ’s

Who is Protect America?

Protect America is the one of the nation’s top security companies providing protection to over 100,000, business customers nationwide.

Why Protect America?

Deciding on the right business security company can be a tough decision. Our goal is to make it easy to choose the right solution by offering products tailored for your business.

Do you offer residential and commercial systems?

No. Protect America only offers services to commercial/business customers through ACN.

What do I need in order to get Protect America/Business services installed at my place of business?

  • Pass a credit check
  • Agree to a 36-month monitoring agreement
  • Place phone order
  • Have access to a computer to e-sign the contract
  • Payment on credit, debit, checking, or savings account
  • Not under contract with any other Security provider

What if I have an existing system?

A Protect America representative can verify if we can take over your existing alarm system

What are the monthly monitoring options?

  • Standard – uses landline telephone to communicate
  • Two-Way Voice – allows direct communication via telephone connection
  • Broadband Interactive – connects via the internet
  • Cellular Interactive

How do I place an order?

  • Call the designated ACN toll free number 800-867-0151.
  • Speak to a Protect America business security consultant.
  • Be in front of a computer, tablet, or smart phone to place payment and e-sign the agreement.

How does the installation process work?

  • Protect America security package will be shipped to the customer.
  • Customer calls in to customer installation department at 888-936-3386 after receipt of the security kit.
  • Protect America, Inc. installation technicians assist the customer over the phone.
  • Installation takes 30 to 45 minutes.

Do I have to sign up for the 36 month contract?

Yes! A 36 month contract is industry standard. Please keep in mind you cannot be under contract with any other security provider.

Can I cancel my contract before 36 months?

Your alarm monitoring service agreement is for 36 months. At the end of 36 months, the agreement will automatically switch to a month to month contract unless you provide at least 30 days written notice before your renewal date.

What if the customer decides to cancel their order?

Once the customer has signed the agreement, they are responsible for the entire length of the agreement; however, a new customer has 14 days from the original shipment date to cancel their service with Protect America. There is a $79.00 restocking fee within the first 14 days.
