Logos

Horizontal

The horizontal logo is the preferred use of our brand.

Vertical

In cases where the horizontal logo becomes illegible we recommend using the vertical logo.

White Logo

In cases where the background is red or blue and the logo becomes hard to read use the white alternative logos.

What Not To Do

It is good practice to use the logo correctly to withhold the integrity of the Protect America brand.

Placing the logo over patterns is not recommended. It obstructs the integrity of the logo.

Do not place the logo over areas where the logo becomes unreadable.

Place the logo on solid color areas.

Place the logo in spaces where there is a solid, clear area.
