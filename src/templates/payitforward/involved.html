Get Involved

The Protect America Pay It Forward Challenge is constantly getting better and growing every year. For the past two collegiate semesters of 2012, Protect America has been involved in helping over 100 college students and over 20 charities. The challenge raises awareness and revenue for charities that don't necessarily have the resources to gain these. The marketing and advertising students gain real world knowledge by helping the charities generate revenue by creating strategic marketing and fundraising campaigns.

Want to get involved?

Anyone can become a part of the Pay It Forward Challenge and help any charity. We encourage you, no matter where you are to pay it forward. If you would like to get involved or have a specific charity you want to help contact us today.
