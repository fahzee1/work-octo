Awareness

To raise awareness, each team will have the option on how extensive their team will be when it comes to marketing for their charity and their company. You can contact local media in addition to building a website, creating a social media campaign, and developing a strong company brand. It's all about getting the word out and watching to see how far it goes. The great thing about awareness is that most of the tools you can use are free. Submit a press release to news stations, contact bloggers, editors, and writers to get a mention or to host your press release. You can also create social media accounts, or revamp existing ones to get the word out. Create a YouTube Channel to host video footage you take from the camera provided by Protect America.
