The 411!

Started in late 2011 at Michigan State University, the PIF Challenge asks marketing and advertising students at major universities to help local charitable organizations raise funds and awareness. At the start of the Challenge, students are broken into teams that devise comprehensive marketing strategies to raise funds and awareness for local charities.

To jump start the Challenge, each team is given a $500 stipend from Protect America. These funds are to be used to create revenue/support and lasting awareness for each participating charity. The success of these initiatives will largely depend on the effort and dedication of each team. To encourage dedication to the Challenge, teams choose their own charity, preferably one that supports a cause near and dear to team members.

The Pay It Forward Challenge is a great opportunity for students to put their education into real world practice. Students gain valuable experience in marketing and advertising while helping charities that serve your local community. Help Protect America bring the Pay It Forward Challenge to your school and make a difference that has a positive impact for everyone involved.

They are able to build an identity and create a team name while responding to the Pay It Forward initiative. Once they create their team and have chosen a charity, they start the challenge by creating awareness and generating funds for the charity. Creatively each team will respond to the challenge differently and create a unique experience. Each team will learn what works for them and will be able to learn and adapt by having limited resources. Protect America is excited to be part of this great challenge and will continue to challenge students for years to come.
