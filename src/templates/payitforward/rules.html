PIFC Rules

The rules for the challenge are quite simple. The students must gather into teams for the challenge. Each team then creates a marketing and advertising agency that will identify their team. Once they create their team brand and identity, they will pick a charity. Each team will then create marketing campaigns to generate funds and awareness for the charity they partner with.

Click to check out last years teams!

  • 1. Teams: Break up into teams, and create your marketing group.
  • 2. Charity: Pick a charity that you will be helping with your team.
  • 3. Planning: Make a plan and create ideas to help the charity. You should develop marketing and fundraising strategies with your team.
  • 4. Reporting: Each team will report their progress in generating awareness and revenue at least once a month. Point Tracking will allow everyone to see where your team stands during the course of the challenge.
  • 5. Presentation: Turn in your final presentation to your professor displaying your team?s accomplishments and creativity during the course of the challenge.
