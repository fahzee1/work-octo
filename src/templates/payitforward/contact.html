Contact Us!

Our mission with the Protect America Cares Pay It Forward Challenge is to provide students a platform to learn from real life experiences that may help them in life and in their careers. We believe helping others brings success and learning is the key. Together we can make a difference and positive impact in our communities in America and beyond. Pay it forward.

If you have a story about you or someone you know that is making a positive impact and paying it forward, we would love to hear from you!

To find out more information on how you can get involved, fill out the contact form below. If you are interested in learning more about the challenge or simply would like some information about Protect America and Protect America Cares, feel free to contact us.
