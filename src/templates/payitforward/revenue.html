Revenue:
$1.00 = 1 pt

Revenue always plays a major role when it comes to charity. This is a great part of the Pay It Forward Challenge and will help decide which team was most effective. Each dollar raised for your charity will earn your team 1 point. While raising awareness for your charity and their cause is most important, we also want to boost their contributions.

Teams can generate revenue a number of ways, such as individual fund-raising events or a donation page on a website. Think of and implement a strategy to generate funds for your charity.

Every team has the same amount of time and opportunity to raise funds. It's all about who will use this time and opportunity to generate the most funds. Be productive and pro active.

*Reporting The dollar amount each team raises for their charity will be tracked on our leader board featured on payitforward

Report the amount of money you have raised at least once a month to Shawne Groves to update your points and make sure they are up to date on the leader board and show off your teams fundraising skills!

*Food and donations will be taken into consideration for points as well, if you are working with a charity that accepts them. Report these items when reporting fundraising money, and awareness points.
