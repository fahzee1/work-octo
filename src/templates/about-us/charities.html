About Protect America

Our Charity Work

As a company, we've made it our goal to help people. Everyday we protect homes and make people feel a little safer where they live. But we strive to be more then a home security company. We are proud to care about those around us and to give back to our community when we can. Below are just some of the people we have helped over the years.

Pay It Forward

Protect America Cares (the charitable organization of home security company, Protect America, Inc.) recently teamed up with students at Michigan State University to raise money and awareness for charities in the Lansing area. Guided by advertising professor David Regan, the students were divided into 7 groups and given $500 seed money from Protect America to create marketing campaigns that help local charities improve fundraising.

Pay It Forward

Ronald McDonald House

Protect America is committed to protecting families and homes across the United States. We feel that the most important thing we can do is keep our children safe. That is why we support Ronald McDonald House and their mission to improve the health and well being of children. We are proud to be one of the largest contributors over the years to the Ronald McDonald House here in Austin, TX.

The Austin Zoo

After finding a full-blooded timberland wolf pup on his property, our CEO, Thad Paschall knew that this small animal would need a special place to call home. With more than 300 animals from more than 100 different species, the Austin Zoo was the perfect place. Austin Zoo is a private, non-profit zoo that rescues animals and serves the Austin community. Here at Protect America, we were proud to have rescued this wolf, affectionately named Joy, and to be involved in building a large and lush wolf habitat for the Austin Zoo. Now, Joy and the other wolves at Austin zoo have a safe place to play hide and seek while enjoying the lazy days of summer.

Austin Pets Alive

Many of us here at Protect America are big animal lovers. And we consider our pets as another important member of our family. So when we found out about the local non-profit, Austin Pets Alive, we knew we had to help. They make it their job to try to find homes for all those pets out there that don't have a family.

Barkin' Doggie Biscuits

Barkin' Doggie Biscuits makes biscuits that your pets will love. And when you buy a treat for your pet, they give one to a dog in need. So, working with various shelters around town, we bought a bunch of these tasty treats so pets in shelters can benefit from this wonderful program!

Feed the Children

Feed The Children is an international, nonprofit relief organization that delivers food, medicine, clothing and other necessities to individuals, children and families who lack these essentials due to famine, war, poverty, or natural disaster. They truly help people when they need it most and Protect America is very proud to support their efforts.

Any Baby Can

Any Baby Can serves more than 6,000 of the youngest, sickest, and poorest children and their families in the Central Texas area. Giving these children the chance to reach their potential through education, therapy and family support services. Protect America is proud to support their endeavors and help children right here in Austin, TX.
