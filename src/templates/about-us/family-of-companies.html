Cellerant Therapeutics

Cellerant Therapeutics is a San Carlos, California-based biopharmaceutical company developing novel therapies for life-threatening blood disorders and cancer. The company's lead product, CLT-008, is currently in two Phase 1 clinical trials in patients with hematological malignancies. Cellerant is also developing CLT-008 as a nuclear countermeasure for treatment of Acute Radiation Syndrome (ARS) under a U.S. Government contract valued at up to $153 million over five years with the Biomedical Advanced Research & Development Authority. There is no FDA-approved medical countermeasure to treat ARS. If licensed by the FDA, the federal government could purchase CLT-008 for the Strategic National Stockpile.
