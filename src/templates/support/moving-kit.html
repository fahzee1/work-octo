EasyMove Moving Kit

Are you moving? Protect America customers who use our GE wireless security system are able to transfer their system and service very easily. Simply fill out this form and we will send you a EasyMove kit for free so you will be able to take your security system with you to your new home. You will be able to use the EasyMove relocation kit to install your system in your new home and then you will need to make sure to contact customer service to update all of your account information and re-activate the system at your new location. If you have questions about moving, please click here or contact one of our security representatives at 1-800-951-5111, Option 4.
