Lifetime Replacement Parts Warranty and Homeowner's Insurance Guarantee

For as long as your home is monitored by Protect America, Inc.'s authorized UL listed monitoring facility, Protect America will provide a Lifetime Replacement Parts Warranty on all Packages AND Protect America will cover up to $250 of your Homeowner's Insurance Deductible in the event of a burglary.

Homeowner's Insurance Discount

Protect America's affordable solutions for your home security and protection are very important. However, another great benefit to having a monitored home security system is that most insurance companies will give you a discount on your homeowner's or renter's insurance premium. After your system is activated, we will provide you with a certificate of installation. You simply provide this certificate to your insurance company as proof that you have a monitored security system, and they will provide you with their standard home security discount.
