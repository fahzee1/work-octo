Door / Window Sensor

These devices are the most common piece of security equipment. Door or window sensors are used to detect when any door or window is opened. The Copper Package is designed to provide basic security by protecting your front door and 2 other doors or windows.

The Silver Package Includes: 10 Door / Window Sensors

Motion Detector

A motion detector is designed to protect the inside of your home. If a burglar somehow bypasses the perimeter sensors, the motion detector will sound the alarm if any movement is detected. We recommend that each motion detector be placed in a heavy traffic area.

The Silver Package Includes: 1 Motion Detector

Yard Sign and Window Decals

Let passersby know that your home is protected with a security yard sign and small but noticeable window stickers. This can make a thief think twice before targeting your home.

The Silver Package Includes: 3 Window Decals and 1 Security Yard Sign

Simon XT Control Panel

The Simon XT is the hub of your entire security system. It is the place you will be entering commands to arm or disarm the system, and we recommend the Simon XT be placed in an easy-to-access location. From there you can arm your system, call emergency response, see important status messages or disarm your system with its keypad.

The Copper Package Includes: 1 Simon XT Control Panel
