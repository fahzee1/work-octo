X-10 Lamp Module

This module is built to control any table lamp, or incandescent lamp from your Simon 3 or Simon XT control panel. Simply plug the Lamp Module into any wall socket, then plug your lamp into the module, and you have wireless versatility. The Lamp Module is an ideal solution for hard-to-reach light switches and rooms with multiple floor lamps or displays.

X-10 Appliance Module

With this extension to your home security system you can automate or remotely control appliances from your Simon 3 or Simon XT control panel. Controllable appliances include: fans, air conditioners, dehumidifiers, coffee pots, stereos, TV's and more.

X-10 Power Horn Module

The power horn module can be added anywhere around your home and communicates with your control panel to produce a loud siren when the alarm is triggered. No intruder will hang around when one of these sirens sound.
