Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should my door be interactive?

    You’ll never be locked out again. Plus, being connected to your home like never before means serious peace of mind. Imagine unlocking doors for a loved one from miles away, or even halfway across the country. Can’t remember if you locked the door on your way out? Check instantly with your smartphone. Give temporary passcodes to visitors and supply loved ones with codes of their own. The possibilities are endless, and life just got a little easier.

  • How do I install my lock?

    Yale Real Living Door Locks are so easy to install, you can do it yourself. A Yale technology expert will guide you through each step of the process, or you can opt to have a locksmith in your area install it for you! Simply call Yale Support at 1-800-810-9473. Yale Support is available from 8:00a.m. – 7:00p.m. (EST) Monday – Friday.

    Once your lock is installed, if you need assistance with programming, interactive control access, or user code programming, please call Protect America Customer Care at 1-800-951-5111 option 4.

  • Can I put this lock on a room door instead of an exterior entry door?

    This lock can be placed on most doors, providing that they are deadbolt compatible. The door thickness must also be within the specifications (1-3/4” to 2-1/4”) for the lock to be installed without an additional gasket. Check with a Protect America security expert and explore your options!

  • How thick must my door be for this lock?

    The Yale Real Living Door Lock can accommodate doors of 1-3/4” to 2-1/4” thickness. If it is to be installed on a 1-3/8’’ to 1-3/4’’ door, a thin door gasket kit will be required to complete installation. Speak with a Protect America security expert to learn more about this technology and its application to your home!

  • How many codes can I program into my lock?

    Program up to 250 codes into your interactive door lock for truly customized access.

  • What is Z-Wave?

    Z-Wave is a home automation technology which lets you control locks, lights, appliances and more remotely with a smartphone, tablet or computer. Z-Wave technology works together with Protect America security systems to transform your house into an interactive, controllable home that is smart and safe. You’re now connected in ways never before possible!

  • Can my lock be unlocked from the interior as well?

    Yes! There is a standard thumbturn knob on the interior side of this lock so you can unlock/lock from the inside of your home.

  • Who do I call for customer support after I install my lock?

    Once your lock is installed, if you need assistance with programming, interactive control access, or user code programming, please call Protect America Customer Care at 1-800-951-5111 option 4.
