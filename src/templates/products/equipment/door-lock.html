Is my home Compatible?

Every home is different. Get all the installation information and requirements before you order

Installation Requirements

Check your existing deadbolt latches

If the edge of the door has 2 individual latches, a Yale Z-Wave Deadbolt Lock can be installed. The longer plates of a Mortise lock or a 3 point locking system could prevent installation.

Measure for available space needed

Check to ensure the hardware below the deadbolt allows enough room for the Yale Z-wave Deadbolt Lock to install properly. There must be at least 2’’ below the deadbolt.

Check to see what may be installed above the existing deadbolt

If no additional locks or hardware interfere with the space required for installation, a Yale Z-wave Deadbolt should install properly.

Check door thickness

If it is between 1-3/4"" and 2-1/4", the lock will work without additional parts. If it is to be installed on a 1-3/8" to 1-3/4"" door, a thin door gasket kit will be required to complete installation.

Check for proper alignment of latches

If the door has to be pushed or pulled with excessive force to lock the existing deadbolt hardware, some adjustments may be required.

An enlarged strike, shown on the left is slightly larger than the existing standard strike on the door. Only use the enlarged strike to correct situations where excessive force is required to lock the existing deadbolt hardware. Yale locks also features a tapered deadbolt which help correct most misalignment conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should my door be interactive?

    You’ll never be locked out again. Plus, being connected to your home like never before means serious peace of mind. Imagine unlocking doors for a loved one from miles away, or even halfway across the country. Can’t remember if you locked the door on your way out? Check instantly with your smartphone. Supply separate user codes for loved ones and guests. The possibilities are endless, and life just got a little easier.

  • How do I install my lock?

    Yale Real Living Door Locks are so easy to install, you can do it yourself. A Yale technology expert will guide you through each step of the process, or you can opt to have a locksmith in your area install it for you! Simply call Yale Support at 1-800-810-9473. Yale Support is available from 8:00a.m. – 7:00p.m. (EST) Monday – Friday.

    Once your lock is installed, if you need assistance with programming, interactive control access, or user code programming, please call Protect America Customer Care at 1-800-951-5111 option 4.

  • How thick must my door be for this lock?

    The Yale Real Living Door Lock can accommodate doors of 1-3/4” to 2-1/4” thickness. If it is to be installed on a 1-3/8’’ to 1-3/4’’ door, a thin door gasket kit will be required to complete installation. Speak with a Protect America security expert to learn more about this technology and its application to your home!

  • Is the lock resistant to "hacking"?

    Yale Real Living’s Z-Wave firmware is highly resistant to a security breach. Yale's wireless Z-Wave adapter sends information out over a 128-bit Advanced Encryption Standard (AES 128). AES 128 is the standard level of encryption for all modern Z-Wave devices, and is also used for things like military communications and online banking transactions.

  • What if my phone gets lost or stolen?

    The SMART Connect app is the portal through which Protect America customers can remotely control their Yale Real Living locks. The most common concern is phones or other mobile devices on which SMART Connect is installed being lost or stolen. We strongly suggest users do the following:

    • Keep devices password protected.
    • As a second measure of defense, users can turn off the “Auto Login” setting on the SMART Connect app login screen. This requires the user to manually input a password when opening the app instead of automatically being granted access.
    • Make sure you have the “Find My iPhone” app on your device. This will allow you to erase your device remotely or put it into “Lost mode” which will lock the device with a passcode. Android users can also do the same with http://wheresmydroid.com
    • If your phone or tablet is lost or stolen, you should immediately go to www.myprotectamerica.com and login to your SMART Connect account and change your password.

    This will prevent anyone from accessing your account from any device, even if you left the Auto Login setting turned on.

  • How many user codes can I program into my lock?

    Program up to 250 user codes into your interactive door lock for truly customized access.

  • What is Z-Wave?

    Z-Wave is a home automation technology which lets you control locks, lights, appliances and more remotely with a smartphone, tablet or computer. Z-Wave technology works together with Protect America security systems to transform your house into an interactive, controllable home that is smart and safe. You’re now connected in ways never before possible!

  • Can my lock be unlocked from the interior as well?

    Yes! There is a standard thumb turn knob on the interior side of this lock so you can unlock/lock from the inside of your home.

  • Who do I call for customer support after I install my lock?

    Once your lock is installed, if you need assistance with programming, interactive control access, or user code programming, please call Protect America Customer Care at 1-800-951-5111 option 4.
