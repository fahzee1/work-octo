When The Door TiltsThe Sensor Activates

The garage door sensor extends intrusion protection to garage structures and enables our customers to achieve whole home security.

By detecting the tilt of the overhead door, the garage door sensor alerts your alarm system if an intruder enters through the garage. Once armed, if the garage door opens to a 45-degree angle, the sensor goes into an alarm condition, which can turn on the siren and automatically call the central monitoring station.
