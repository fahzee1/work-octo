This little device gives you access to many of your security system's controls including arming, disarming, and panic mode. It is important that this device is in a secure or hidden location. With the provided screws or double-side foam, you can pretty much hide it anywhere. Below are some suggestions on how to use and hide the secret keypad.

No Code Needed

The secret wireless keypad is programmed specifically for your alarm system and has direct access to arming and disarming your system. Therefore, you don't have to remember any alarm codes or passwords. Just put it in a secret location and arm your home by pressing a button.

In Case of Emergency

Pressing and holding the Arm and Disarm buttons for two seconds sets your alarm in panic mode. This mode instantly sets off your alarm and contacts our monitoring station. Hiding a secret wireless keypad in an area you or your children might hide if a burglar enters your home is perfect for access to the alarm even if you can't get to the main control panel.

Disarm your alarm when you arrive home.

Hiding the secret wireless keypad in the garage allows you to disarm the system when you arrive home from work. Just get out of your car and disarm the system on your way in the door.

Convenient Control - Anywhere

With its slim design, the secret wireless keypad can be placed anywhere you might need an extra control panel. This means that you can put it in a place that is convenient for you. We suggest you find a spot that is hidden but you frequently pass. The keypad easily fits inside a drawer or cabinet. With the provided foam pads, you can stick the keypad pretty much wherever you can think of.

Worried about your kids?

Placing the secret wireless keypad in a place that your children can reach (e.g. a low cabinet) allows them to arm and disarm the system when they arrive home from school. The keypad makes it easy to keep them secure within your home without the need to remember any codes or special passwords. Also, with our security system's pager features allow you to automatically receive a message when your children arrive home.

Arming Before Bed

Placing the wireless secret keypad in a drawer or the side of a nightstand gives you access to your system right before bed and when waking up in the morning. As you head to bed, make sure your system is locked and secured with a simple button press. Hear a crash in the night? With the optional X-10 components, use it as a convenient extra light switch to flip the lights on in the house.

Panic Room Button

In case a burglar does enter your home, have a pre-planned location like a closet or even a panic room for you or your child to hide. Placing the secret keypad in this location will allow you to set off the alarm and send out a duress signal.
