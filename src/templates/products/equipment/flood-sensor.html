Water Damage Can Be Costly.Be Preventative And Get Flood Sensors

The Flood Sensor is designed to discover any leakage in the part of your home or business that’s most susceptible to water and flooding. With prompt notice it affords you ample space and time to salvage your valuables when you encounter problems with water damage and it saves you time and money.

The Flood Sensor contains an 8-foot cord that is connected to the detector device. Once water is detected an alarm signal is transmitted.

The transmitter in the flood sensor has a functional span (minimally) of 500 feet in free space (depending on location and the kind of control panel you have). The flood sensor battery will last about 3 to 5 years.

Flood Sensor

The basement or first level of your business or residence (i.e. in the kitchen if you don’t have a basement) is the best place to locate you Flood Sensor. Mount the detector on the lowermost area of your wall or even right on the floor in order to promptly detect high water or low water.
