Stopping the Silent Killer

The wireless carbon monoxide detector is an important safety tool that alerts you of dangerous levels of carbon monoxide (CO) in your home. Carbon Monoxide is a colorless, odorless, and tasteless gas that is harmful to you and your pets. When potentially dangerous levels of CO are present, it provides a warning signal to alert you and Protect America for help. The alarm will only reset once dangerous carbon monoxide levels are no longer being detected.

In The Home:

Carbon Monoxide

Carbon Monoxide Alarms should be mounted on all levels of your home, and are best situated in bedrooms and living rooms. In other words, you should be able to hear the alarm from wherever you and your family typically sleep, NOT near a fireplace, furnace or in the basement (unless of course someone sleeps in the basement).
