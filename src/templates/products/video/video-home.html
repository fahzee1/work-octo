Get the Full, Clear, Unpixelated Picture

With traditional video cameras, you are stuck with a small fixed viewing area. And lower resolution cameras mean you get an unrecognizable, pixelated image. So when creating our home security video services, we built in pan and tilt capabilities with every camera.
