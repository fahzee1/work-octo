    Monitoring Options

    Having a home security system installed is just the first half of a secured home. When your alarm goes off, the security system sends out a signal to our monitoring station which, in turn, contacts you and the authorities. Without a home security monitoring service, an alarm that is tripped never contacts the police. We are proud to offer a variety of monitoring solutions to fit your home, lifestyle, and budget.

    • Cellular Monitoring

      The control panel is built with a cellular device that directly contacts authorities in the event of an alarm.

    • Broadband Monitoring

      Using your broadband modem or router, the alarm panel contacts authorities and dispatches in the event of an alarm.

    • Landline Monitoring

      Using a standard telephone line, the control panel directly contacts authorities in the event of an alarm.
